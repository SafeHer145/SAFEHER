About

SafeHer enables quick SOS to up to five verified contacts via native SMS, including your live GPS location as a Google Maps link. Core flows work without internet. Email verification is required before account use.

How to Install

  1. Tap Download Android APK above.
  2. On your phone, allow installs from your browser (Unknown sources).
  3. Open the downloaded APK and follow the install prompts.

Key Features

  • SOS SMS with GPS location
  • Add and verify up to 5 contacts
  • Offline‑first design (native SMS works without internet)
  • Firebase Auth + Firestore logging
  • English, Hindi, Telugu
